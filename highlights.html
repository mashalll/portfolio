Projects



Care on Click

Care on Click was a charitable initiative I took part in creating for MetroHacks. This website would provide care packages with toiletries and snacks to help workers get through their long shifts, financial aid, PPE donations and other mental health resources, all community driven.

Kym Nylz

As part of HerVolution's STEMing Up Program, I was involved in the development of Kym Nylz's e-commerce website as a web developer intern. I was part of a team of developers and a designer, working together to build this website on Shopify.

Tractus

Tractus was a women's safety app I developed with a team for the Technovation hackathon. I used the Thunkable platform and Firebase authentication to create a responsive app with features to help in danger.
